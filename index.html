About the Doctor

A Gold Medalist Physiotherapist

Dr. Aarthi Ganesh is the founder and chief physiotherapist of Shree Physiotherapy Clinic. A distinguished gold medalist in physiotherapy, she has earned recognition for her exceptional clinical skills and patient-centered approach. Her relentless pursuit of excellence led her to Italy, where she obtained her prestigious Fascial Manipulation certification, making her one of the few certified practitioners in the region.

With over a decade of experience, Dr. Aarthi has established herself as the best physiotherapist in Periyanaickenpalayam and surrounding areas. She specializes in women's health physiotherapy, providing compassionate care for pre-natal, post-natal, and other women-specific conditions. Her dedicated elderly home care services ensure that senior patients receive expert treatment in the comfort of their homes.
