Understanding Fascial Manipulation: The Italian Technique That's Transforming Pain Treatment

Fascial Manipulation is a revolutionary manual therapy technique developed by Luigi Stecco in Italy. Unlike traditional massage or physiotherapy, this method targets the fascial system - the three-dimensional web of connective tissue that surrounds every muscle, bone, nerve, and organ in your body.

At Shree Physiotherapy Clinic, Dr. Aarthi Ganesh is one of the few certified Fascial Manipulation practitioners in South India, having trained directly in Italy. This technique has shown remarkable results in treating:

  • Chronic back pain and sciatica
  • Frozen shoulder and rotator cuff injuries
  • Knee pain and osteoarthritis
  • Neck pain and cervical spondylosis
  • Fibromyalgia and chronic fatigue

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about physiotherapy answered by Dr. Aarthi Ganesh

What is Fascial Manipulation therapy?

Fascial Manipulation is an advanced manual therapy technique developed in Italy that targets the fascial system - the connective tissue network throughout your body. Dr. Aarthi Ganesh is one of the few certified practitioners in South India, trained directly in Italy. This technique provides immediate pain relief and restores mobility by releasing restrictions in the fascial network.

How can physiotherapy help with back pain?

Physiotherapy is highly effective for back pain treatment. At Shree Physiotherapy Clinic, we use a combination of manual therapy, fascial manipulation, therapeutic exercises, and posture correction to address the root cause of back pain. Most patients experience significant relief within 3-5 sessions.

Do you provide home physiotherapy services?

Yes, Dr. Aarthi Ganesh provides home physiotherapy services for elderly patients and those unable to visit the clinic. We cover Periyanaickenpalayam, Karamadai, and surrounding areas in Coimbatore. Home visits include comprehensive assessment and treatment with the same quality care as clinic visits.

How many sessions do I need for recovery?

The number of sessions varies based on your condition. Acute injuries may resolve in 3-5 sessions, while chronic conditions might need 10-15 sessions. Dr. Aarthi Ganesh will assess your condition and provide a personalized treatment plan with expected timeline during your first visit.

What conditions does women's health physiotherapy treat?

Women's health physiotherapy at Shree Physiotherapy Clinic covers pre-natal and post-natal care, pelvic floor dysfunction, diastasis recti, pregnancy-related back pain, urinary incontinence, and menopause-related musculoskeletal issues. Dr. Aarthi provides compassionate, specialized care in a private setting.

Is physiotherapy painful?

While some techniques may cause mild discomfort, physiotherapy should not be painful. Dr. Aarthi Ganesh uses gentle, patient-centered approaches and always communicates throughout the treatment. The goal is healing with comfort.
